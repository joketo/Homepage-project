Your project

Do you have a material that you love but don't yet know what to make of it? Or do you have a specific idea of your dream garment?
I sew high-quality garments and costumes for all your needs. I also offer single costumes for theatre or help with the overall costuming.
I work in Helsinki, Finland and most commissions require meeting face to face. However, simple designs like skirts or capes are possible to make completely remotely.
For more information, please contact me by email johanna@wahtera.fi or by filling the form below.
