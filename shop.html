Ready-made clothes

I will launch my fantasy-inspired collection on etsy.com soon. These clothes are unique and fixed size so please check the measurements carefully before buying!
To make sure that the pieces fit you, please send me the following measurements:
  • Height
  • Bust
  • Waist
  • Hip
  • Arm width and length (if the design includes sleeves)
I will tell you if the garment can be altered to fit your measurements. Please note that altered clothes are custom made and cannot be returned.
The designs can often be modified for an extra fee. Tell me your wishes and we'll see what can be done!
