Everyone has art portfolios, but why are there programming projects in mine?

At the end of highschool I got interested in programming. When I needed to decide what I wanted to study, I chose Computer Science at the University of Helsinki. At that time I didn't really know what it was all about and spent the first year mostly being very confused. Everyone seemed to be so good already and I was just getting started - was this really something I could do?

After three years I decided it was time to try something else and started studies to be a craft teacher. It was something so compeletly different that I thought it would balance out the theory-heavy CS-studies. For a while (a month, really) I felt that teaching was definately my field. Nonetheless, I soon found myself missing programming and all the challenge, because that's what computer science for me is all about. I decided to give it one last try and started completing my bachelor's degree. Something had clicked and everything seemed much more interesting and compelling all of the sudden.

I'm now finishing my last mathematics course required for my bachelor's degree. There is still much I want to learn! But as the title of my Code-page says, I've taken the first steps.
