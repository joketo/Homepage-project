Ecological Enchantment

About the Maker

My name is Johanna Wahtera, lovely to meet you! I have admired beautiful clothes as long as I remember. I'm a qualified sewing teacher and enjoy creating clothes with a magical touch. I'm currently living in Finland.

Individual Designs

I get inspiration for my pieces from fairy tales, history and nature. However, what drives my designs the most are the materials I find. All my clothes are unique because I aspire to use materials that have already been used once.

Why Recycled Materials

Let's face it, textile production is not good for our planet. To reduce environmental impact the produced materials should be used as long as possible. This is the reason why I use mostly recycled fabrics on my clothes. I hunt down materials from second hand stores with love. Old home textiles are a great example of re-usable materials.

I always check the fabricâ€™s condition with care before sewing. To assure their freshness and color resistance I freeze and wash them after acquiring them. As a bonus this makes sure that the finished product is not going to shrink when the customer washes it.

I include care instructions for every clothing I sell. I hope that the new owner will handle the product with care so that the materials used in it will have a long, happy life.

This is my small contribution in the fight against the fast-fashion industry and harmful consumption. With my clothes, I hope to make the world a slightly better place.
