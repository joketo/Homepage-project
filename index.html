About the Maker
My name is Johanna Wahtera, lovely to meet you! I'm a qualified sewing teacher and enjoy creating clothes with an enchanted twist. I craft them in a pet and smoke free workroom in Finland.
Individual Designs
I get inspiration for my pieces from fairy tales, history and nature. However, what drives my designs the most are the materials I find. Because I mainly use recycled fabrics, I usually have enough material for only a one piece of clothing. This results to unique, one of a kind designs.
Why Reused Materials
Let's face it, textile production is not good for our planet. To reduce environmental impact the produced materials should be used as long as possible. My way of reinforcing this is to reuse old fabrics on my clothes.
I always check the fabricâ€™s condition with care before sewing. To assure their freshness and color resistance I freeze and wash them after acquiring them. As a bonus this makes sure that the finished product is not going to shrink when the new owner washes it.
This is my small contribution in the fight against the fast-fashion industry and harmful consumption. With my clothes, I hope to bring joy to my customers and prolong the life of otherwise wasted fabrics.
